I am the Vice President of a newly made Engineering Program called Path 2 Engineering(P2E). I am very passionate about going into the STEM field and for this reason I wanted to take part in helping other people who have the same the same goal I have in any way I can by being the Vice President. P2E's goal is to be a safe space in which members can discover their love for engineering and a where creativity can be expressed freely with no judgment. Our vision for Pathways 2 Engineering next year is devoted to different types of engineering.

The Neubauer Phoenix STEM Summer Program included me spending one week on the UChicago campus. After I completed an online mathematics course, I resided on campus for one week and explored the opportunities available to me by being at UChicago. Through a week of taking real college courses, meeting college professors, and experiencing facility tours, I had the opportunity to hear from world-renowned UChicago faculty in molecular engineering, mathematics, computer science, and other fields.
